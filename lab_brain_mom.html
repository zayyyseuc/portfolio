Contextual Sharing

A sharing model where the object is not a post, photo, or caption, but a bounded slice of personal context that can explain itself.

Notes raw thinking, references, unfinished ideas
State recent activity, mood, milestones, tasks
Media photos, traces, small moments
AI

Context snapshot what matters now, without the whole archive
Explainable update summary, source, tone, receiver-friendly framing
Follow-up space questions continue from selected context

Why AI is necessary

Retrieval Find the relevant pieces inside a messy personal archive.
Compression Turn dense traces into something another person can read quickly.
Translation Adapt tone, detail, and framing to the receiver.
Responsiveness Let the shared window answer follow-up questions without exposing everything.
Not more sharing. More meaningful access.
